Established in 1900, Soochow University (SCU) is one of Taiwan's oldest private universities. Located in Taipei, SCU boasts a rich history and a commitment to academic excellence. Renowned for its School of Law and School of Business, the university offers a diverse range of undergraduate and graduate programs across various disciplines. SCU's campus provides a vibrant learning environment with modern facilities and beautiful surroundings.

SCU emphasizes global perspectives and social responsibility, encouraging students to engage in international exchanges, research collaborations, and community service activities. With a focus on interdisciplinary learning and innovation, SCU prepares students for success in the global marketplace and fosters lifelong learning.

The university's faculty comprises distinguished scholars and researchers who are dedicated to mentorship and academic excellence. SCU is committed to promoting cultural diversity, intellectual curiosity, and ethical leadership among its students, empowering them to make meaningful contributions to society.
